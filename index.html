Doraemon: Ek Safar

Manga Start: Dec 1969 | Anime 1979-2026 | Legacy Farewell: 2026

1. Doraemon Ka Itihas (Origin)

Doraemon ek robot cat hai jo 22nd century se aaya hai. Uska janm **Matsushiba Robot Factory** mein hua tha. Asliyat mein Doraemon ka rang **Yellow** tha aur uske kaan the, lekin ek robotic mouse ne uske kaan kha liye. Is dard se wo itna roya ki uski yellow skin nikal gayi aur wo **Blue** ho gaya. Use **Sewashi Nobi** (Nobita ka great-great-grandson) ne past mein bheja taaki wo Nobita ki galtiyan sudhar sake.

2. Nobita aur uske Dost (Main Characters)

Nobita Nobi
Co-protagonist jo padhai mein kamzor hai par shooting aur string figure mein mahir hai.
Shizuka Minamoto
Nobita ki best friend jise bathing aur sweet potatoes pasand hain.
Takeshi "Gian" Goda
Bully jiska gaana sabke liye nightmare hai par wo doston ka sath deta hai.
Suneo Honekawa
Rich spoiled kid jo apne gadgets dikhata hai par dil ka bura nahi hai.

Supporting roles mein **Dorami** (Doraemon ki behen) aur **Hidetoshi Dekisugi** (Nobita ka intelligent rival) shamil hain.

3. Magical Tools (Iconic Gadgets)

Doraemon ke 4-dimensional pocket mein futuristic department store ke hazaron tools hain:

  • Anywhere Door (Doko Demo Doa): Kahin bhi pal bhar mein pahunchne ke liye.
  • Bamboo Copter (Take-copter): Aasmaan mein udne ke liye.
  • Time Machine: Nobita ki desk drawer ke andar jo past aur future le jati hai.
  • Big Light/Small Light: Kisi bhi cheez ka size badalne ya chota karne ke liye.
